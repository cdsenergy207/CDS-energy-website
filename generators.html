Why Maine Homeowners Install Standby Generators

Maine winters bring ice storms, heavy snow, and extended power outages. A standby generator keeps your home safe, warm, and fully operational when the grid goes down. CDS Energy Services provides professional installation, replacement, and maintenance across Maine.

Heating & Safety

Keep your heating system running during freezing temperatures and prevent dangerous frozen pipes.

Basement Flooding

Keep sump pumps running during heavy storms to prevent costly water damage.

Medical Essentials

Ensure uninterrupted power for essential medical devices like CPAP machines and oxygen equipment.

Protect Food

Keep refrigerators and freezers running during extended outages to prevent food loss.

Stay Connected

Keep lights, internet, and phones running so you can stay connected during outages.

Peace of Mind

Automatic standby systems detect outages and restore power within seconds.
